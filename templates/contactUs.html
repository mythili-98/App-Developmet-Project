The ETA provided is a general guide. Items are subject to variation depending on multiple factors including customs inspections.
However, if your shipment did not arrive after the ETA by 1 to 2 weeks, please contact our hot-line at 61234567 for assistance.

Service Fee is 2.14% (GST included) of the total payment of an order after application of any vouchers/discounts, including shipping fees. It will be rounded up to 2 decimal places to added to your total fee.

  • Credit/Debit Card
    • Card payments are processed through third-party providers, we currently support Visa & Mastercard.
  • Bank Transfer
    • Buyers can perform an ATM transfer or i-Banking transfer or through the AXS machine to our designated SOBERful bank account.
  • Credit Card Installment Plan
    • Installment plan is processed through third-party providers, we currently support 6 and 12 month plans with the following banks:
    DBS, UOB & OCBC.

You can instantly cancel your order within an hour after your payment is verified and/or before the seller arranges pickup/enters the tracking number. If a paid order is canceled, we will automatically process a refund to your account.

An ETA is shown at the product page when adding to cart and you may not amend the international shipping method of the item.
We will try our best to deliver all the items to you on or before ETA. We will monitor and ship out in around 1 working day after
items under the same parcel are Ready for Shipment. An updated ETA will be provided upon shipment.
